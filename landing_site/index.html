Proof of View, Click, and Actions

Reduce dispute among advertisers, ad companies, and ad networks

Advertisement campaign tracking and attribution is a very important business issue for advertisers, ad companies, and ad networks. Occasional disputes among companies can occur and proof is difficult. We use Nebulas to track advertisement campaigns with different solutions for CPC (click), CPM (view) and CPA (action).

We use Nebulas blockchain's immutable decentralized ledger, smart contract, and NAS token to record customer interaction with served Ad and verify payment amount.

Tracking on Decentralized Ledger

Natural fit for Nebulas

The smart contract tracking provides proof to publisher, ad networks, and companies. Nebulas is a natural fit for the problem due to NR (Nebulas Rank) and NI (Nebulas Incentive). In our unique and innovative design, a good advertisement campaign will bring higher Nebulas Ranks to advertising account running the campaign. Finally, the company running the tracking service gets Nebulas Incentives in their smart contract.
